About DOS-E&A

Enterprise expertise.
Boutique accountability.

DOS Engineering & Automation (DOS-E&A) is a multi-discipline engineering consultancy serving complex industrial operations across Australia. We provide independent technical expertise across electrical engineering, automation and control systems, and multi-discipline project delivery.

Our project experience spans bulk material handling machinery, processing plants, industrial substations, and automated production infrastructure — environments where engineering rigour, safety, and on-time delivery are non-negotiable.

DOS-E&A operates as a true consultancy — independent of any vendor, integrator, or OEM. We advise, design, and deliver. You work directly with the engineer doing the work.

What We Do

Our Services

From concept to commissioning, DOS-E&A delivers across the full project lifecycle — in environments where performance, safety, and reliability are non-negotiable.

01

Engineering Consultancy & Advisory

Independent technical advisory, feasibility studies, concept and FEED-level design across electrical, automation, and control disciplines. We help you make the right decisions before you commit capital.

02

Electrical Engineering & Infrastructure

Detailed electrical design for industrial facilities — HV and LV power distribution, substations, MCCs, switchboards, and site power infrastructure. From single machines to plant-wide systems.

03

Automation & Control Systems

PLC, SCADA, and DCS design and integration for industrial machinery and process infrastructure. Software developed to IEC 61131-3, with safety architectures to ISO 13849-1 and IEC 62061.

04

Project Management & Turnkey Delivery

Multi-discipline project execution across bulk material handling machinery, processing plants, and industrial infrastructure. Business case through to commissioning — one point of accountability.

05

Smart Retrofits & Industrial IoT

Unlock data from existing plant and machinery. Remote monitoring, predictive maintenance, and real-time analytics — without a full system replacement.

06

Custom Software & Product Development

Bespoke HMI and SCADA visualisation, cloud-based reporting, and purpose-built embedded hardware and firmware for industrial applications.

About

Built on real-world engineering.

DOS-E&A is led by a Chartered Professional Engineer (RPEQ, CPEng, IntPE, NER, FSEng TÜV Rheinland) with over a decade of hands-on experience delivering multi-discipline engineering on some of Australia's most demanding industrial projects.

Our background spans electrical engineering, automation and control systems, and multi-discipline project management — across bulk material handling machinery, processing plants, industrial substations, and automated production infrastructure. That breadth is rare in a consultancy of our size, and it means we can engage meaningfully across the full scope of a project — not just one discipline in isolation.

Our philosophy: we don't over-engineer and we don't over-promise. We give you independent advice and deliver robust, maintainable systems built for the realities of industrial operations. Not tied to any vendor — we recommend what's right for your project.

  • Electrical Engineering HV and LV power distribution, industrial substations, MCC and switchboard engineering, protection and control, and site power infrastructure for processing plants and bulk handling facilities.
  • Automation & Control Systems PLC, SCADA, and DCS engineering for industrial machinery and process infrastructure. IEC 61131-3 software development. Architectures aligned to ISA-88, ISA-95, ISA-98, and ISA/IEC 62443 cybersecurity principles.
  • Functional Safety Safety system design and verification to ISO 13849-1, IEC 62061, and AS 4024. FSEng (TÜV Rheinland) certified. SIL determination, SISTEMA analysis, and safety-rated control system integration.
  • Bulk Handling & Processing Plant Multi-discipline engineering for large bulk material handling machines, conveyor systems, processing plants, and associated electrical and control infrastructure.
  • Multi-Discipline Project Management End-to-end project delivery across electrical, automation, and mechanical disciplines — from feasibility and detailed design through procurement, commissioning, and handover.
